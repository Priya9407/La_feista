Our Story

A little corner of Italy, made for Chennai

La Festa began as a family kitchen with one wood-fired oven and a simple promise: every plate should feel like it was made for someone we love.

Today that promise hasn't changed. Our dough is proofed for 24 hours, our sauces simmer all morning, and our desserts are made in-house, never store-bought. From quick weeknight pizzas to long, lazy family dinners, La Festa is built around the table — cozy booths, warm lighting, and a kitchen that stays open long after most others have closed.

  • Fresh, daily ingredients — sourced and prepped each morning
  • Homemade desserts — waffles, brownies & gelato made in-house
  • Late-night dining — open well past midnight on weekends
  • Family-first seating — booths, group tables & a kids-friendly menu
